Welcome to East Leigh Sunhali Himal Prep. School, Dalanwala!


Previous Next

The East Leigh Prep School was founded in July 1986 by the Late Col Ram Singh Thapa and is an English Medium preparatory School from Playgroup to Class VIII following the C.B.S.E pattern in line with BSA Uttarakhand fully Registered and Recognized. Currently the school has about 300 Students and a Staff of 20 well qualified Teachers. The school has all the facilities which are required for over-all development of children which includes Computer Labs, HCL Smart Learning classes, spacious ventilated class rooms, music, dance and art-craft rooms along with a play area and badminton and basket ball courts for sports and recreation. The staff are all well qualified headed by Principal Mrs Alka Gupta with an average Teacher Student ratio of 1:25.

The School is located at Circular Road Dalanwala just opposite Welham Girls High school and in fact is surrounded on 3 sides by Welham Girls School. Adjoining areas include other very prestigious schools like the Welham Boys School, Colonel Brown’s School and the Doon international School. Also Dalanwala is one of the most serene, green and affluent areas in Dehradun.

About Us!


The school was established for the purpose of providing good English medium education at very nominal rates for the children of the economically weaker/under privileged sections of the society. Although Dalanwala is the most affluent location in Dehradun, yet just less than 1 km away there are several slum areas having a population of several thousand where quality education for the children is still a dream because the parents cannot afford to send their children to a good English medium school. Seeing this, the school was established for the purpose of providing good education at a very low cost for families who can barely afford to pay the fees. Also some of the families who could not pay the fees were taken in completely free.
